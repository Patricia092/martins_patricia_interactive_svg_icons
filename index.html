The Summer Flavors

Zima, a clear malt beverage, has been popular for many years. With its unique and refreshing taste, Zima has gained a loyal following among consumers. In addition to the previous flavors, Zima now comes in six additional fruit flavors: strawberry, apple, watermelon, kiwi, orange, and blueberry. Each flavor has a unique taste that is both refreshing and light, making it an ideal beverage for hot summer days.
The strawberry flavor has a sweet and fruity taste that is reminiscent of a summer picnic. The apple flavor provides a crisp and refreshing taste that is perfect for a hot day. The watermelon flavor is juicy and sweet, making it a perfect choice for a summer barbecue. The kiwi flavor has a tangy and tropical taste that is perfect for a day at the beach. The orange flavor is sweet and citrusy, providing a refreshing flavor. Finally, the blueberry flavor provides a sweet and tart taste that is perfect for a summer evening.
The Zima summer flavors provide a refreshing and delicious alternative to traditional summer drinks, and their unique taste profiles make them stand out from other malt beverages. Whether you are a fan of the original Zima or are trying it for the first time, the summer flavors are definitely worth a try. So, go ahead and grab a six-pack of Zima summer flavors and enjoy a refreshing and tasty beverage this summer!
